# The Confident Footballer - Website PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a website for The Confident Footballer - a mindset programme for young footballers (soccer).

### Requirements:
- Services: Online Course, Individual Mentorships, Ebook (with external checkout links)
- Testimonial gallery with photos of soccer players and quotes from parents
- Hero section with provided banner image
- About section explaining what the programme does
- Diego section - the specialist behind the programme
- White background theme (user preference)
- Black and red brand colors
- Sports/athletic typography

## User Personas
1. **Parents of Young Footballers** - Primary audience looking to help their children build mental strength and confidence
2. **Young Players (U12-U16)** - Secondary audience who may browse the site themselves

## Core Requirements
- Single-page landing site with smooth scroll navigation
- External links to checkout pages (no payment processing needed)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clean, white background with black/red accents
- Athletic typography (Barlow Condensed for headings)
